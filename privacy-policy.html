1. Introduction

VedaWell ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

We request access to your device's location to calculate accurate astronomical data (Panchang), sunrise/sunset times, and localized astrological predictions. This data is processed locally on your device or sent securely to our servers for calculation and is not shared with third parties for advertising.

2.2 Notifications

We use notification permissions to send you daily wellness reminders, ritual alerts, and festival notifications. You can opt-out of these at any time in the App settings.

2.3 User Preferences

We store your preferences (such as selected language, birth details for horoscope) locally on your device to provide a personalized experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide accurate astronomical and astrological calculations
  • To send timely reminders for your selected rituals
  • To improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • To personalize your daily horoscope based on birth details

4. Data Security

🛡️ Your data is protected

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (e.g., Firebase by Google) to facilitate our Service. These third parties have access to your device ID and usage data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
