🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

TL;DR: VedaWell Tools runs entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Introduction

Welcome to VedaWell Tools ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at vedawelltools.netlify.app (the "Website").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our tools are designed to work entirely in your browser without sending your data to any external servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Unlike many websites, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Any data you enter into our tools
  • Your IP address or location

3. How Our Tools Work

All our tools process data locally in your web browser:

  • Calculators: Calculations happen in your browser
  • Converters: Conversions are processed locally
  • Generators: All generation happens client-side
  • Image Tools: Images are never uploaded to our servers
  • Games: High scores are stored only in your browser's localStorage

4. Local Storage

Some of our tools use your browser's localStorage to save preferences and data:

  • Game high scores
  • Todo lists and notes
  • User preferences (dark mode, etc.)

This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may include:

  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google's servers. Google may log basic request data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • External Links: We link to third-party sites (Buy Me a Coffee, Gumroad). These sites have their own privacy policies.

6. Advertising

We may display advertisements on our website in the future. If we do, we will update this policy to reflect any changes in data handling. Currently, we use placeholder ad spots that do not collect any data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Security

Since we don't collect or store your data, there's minimal security risk from our side. However, we still implement security best practices:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • Security headers (X-Frame-Options, CSP, etc.)
  • No external scripts that could compromise your data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

📧 Email: support@vedawellapp.com

Note: This privacy policy applies to the VedaWell Tools website only. For the VedaWell mobile app, please refer to the app's privacy policy at vedawellapp.com/privacy-policy
